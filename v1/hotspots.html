Must-Visit Hotspots

Explore the iconic destinations that make New Hampshire unforgettable.

White Mountain National Forest

Experience breathtaking views, hike the majestic Mount Washington, and explore scenic driving routes. A paradise for outdoor enthusiasts year-round.

Lake Winnipesaukee

The largest lake in the state, offering boating, swimming, and charming lakeside towns. Perfect for family getaways and summer relaxation.

Portsmouth & The Seacoast

Discover historic brick sidewalks, incredible dining, and coastal beauty. Stroll through Prescott Park or visit the Strawberry Banke Museum.
